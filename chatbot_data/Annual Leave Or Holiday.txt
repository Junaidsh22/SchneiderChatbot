Annual Leave Entitlement

Q: What are the annual leave entitlements?
Q: How many holiday days do I get?

Q: How many days off am I entitled to per year?
Q: How much annual leave do we get at Schneider?
Q: What is the holiday entitlement for staff?
Q: How many days of leave do we have including bank holidays?
Q: How many days can I take off annually?

A: Employees receive 28 days of annual leave per year (including bank holidays). The leave year runs from 1 January to 31 December, and leave should be taken within the year unless approved otherwise. Up to 5 days may be carried over with manager/HR approval.

Notice Period for Taking Leave
