### IPA SharePoint – Full Directory of Pages & Their Purpose

A:
Below is the complete directory of pages currently published on the IPA SharePoint (as of the last chatbot update – Dec 2025). Each page supports different functions across governance, IT, HR, collaboration, onboarding, and project delivery:

• **UK & Ireland – Main Page**  
  The central landing page for UK&I operations. Hosts key announcements, policies, updates, and essential resources for employees.

• **Export Control COE Group**  
  Dedicated hub for export compliance. Includes export control policies, guidelines, approval workflows, and required documentation.

• **Data Security**  
  Contains Schneider Electric data protection policies, security best practices, encryption standards, and compliance resources.

• **Global Spice+**  
  Main collaboration platform portal. Provides governance documents, user guides, project tools, and ongoing feature updates.

• **ITDocs Group**  
  Repository of IT troubleshooting guides, setup instructions, IT policies, and technical support documentation.

• **NextGen Development Framework**  
  Standards and guidelines for development teams. Includes coding practices, architecture principles, and best-practice documentation.

• **Designer**  
  A resource library for design teams. Includes branding guidelines, templates, creative assets, and design standards.

• **Learning Site on Spice+**  
  Central hub for e-learning. Includes courses, certification paths, skill-boosting modules, and training materials.

• **Endpoint (Enterprise Mobility)**  
  Guides for managing corporate mobile devices and endpoints. Includes setup steps, security requirements, and compliance rules.

• **Industry End User & SI Campus**  
  Knowledge hub for industry-specific content, system integrator onboarding, case studies, and technical documentation.

• **Global HR**  
  Home of global HR policies, forms, benefits information, onboarding details, and HR-related guidance.

• **SE UK&I Graduate Community Group**  
  Community page for graduates. Includes events, development programs, networking opportunities, and shared resources.

• **IC Classic Video Repository**  
  Library for instructional, corporate, and training videos, including webinars and product demonstrations.

• **Pages**  
  A structured container that stores general content pages and miscellaneous internal documentation.

• **UK & I Sales Excellence Group**  
  Supports sales teams with performance dashboards, KPIs, playbooks, and sales enablement materials.

• **UK&I Onboarding Hub Group**  
  The main onboarding centre for new hires. Includes welcome packs, mandatory training, guidance pages, and checklists.

• **Grads Planner Group**  
  Planning tools for graduates. Contains event schedules, calendars, development timelines, and task trackers.

• **Spice+ Strategy & Sustainability**  
  Communicates Schneider Electric corporate strategy, sustainability goals, reports, dashboards, and progress updates.

• **All Company Group**  
  Main communication hub for global announcements, leadership updates, newsletters, and company-wide messages.

This directory forms the backbone of the IPA SharePoint, giving employees structured access to policies, documentation, training, governance content, and collaboration tools.
